## Introduction

The VaporFund Referral Program rewards users for inviting friends and community members to the platform. By sharing your unique referral link, you can earn ongoing rewards based on your referees' staking activity.

### Key Benefits

- **Passive Income**: Earn rewards from your referees' staking activities
- **No Limits**: Unlimited number of referrals
- **Transparent Tracking**: Real-time dashboard showing all referral activity
- **Instant Attribution**: Referrals automatically linked to your account
- **Lifetime Rewards**: Continue earning as long as your referees remain active

## How It Works

### 1. Generate Your Referral Link

Once you've connected your wallet to VaporFund:

1. Navigate to the **Referrals** section in your dashboard
2. Your unique referral link is automatically generated
3. Copy and share your link via social media, email, or messaging apps

**Example Referral Link Format:**
```
https://staking.vaporfund.com?ref=0xYourWalletAddress
```

### 2. Share Your Link

Share your referral link with:

- Friends and family interested in DeFi
- Community members in Discord, Telegram, or Twitter
- Your blog or YouTube channel audience
- Email newsletter subscribers

### 3. Earn Rewards

When someone:

1. Clicks your referral link
2. Connects their wallet
3. Stakes tokens on VaporFund

You'll automatically start earning referral rewards based on their staking activity.

## Referral Rewards

### Reward Structure

| Tier | Referral Count | Commission Rate | Bonus |
|------|----------------|----------------|-------|
| **Bronze** | 1-10 referrals | 5% | - |
| **Silver** | 11-50 referrals | 7% | +2% on all previous referrals |
| **Gold** | 51-100 referrals | 10% | +5% on all previous referrals |
| **Platinum** | 101+ referrals | 15% | +10% on all previous referrals |

### How Rewards Are Calculated

Referral rewards are calculated based on your referees' **staking rewards**:

```
Your Referral Reward = Referee's Staking Reward √ó Your Commission Rate
```

**Example:**

- Your referee stakes 1,000 USDC
- They earn 50 USDC in staking rewards (5% APY)
- Your commission rate is 10% (Gold Tier)
- **You earn: 50 √ó 10% = 5 USDC**

### Reward Distribution

- **Frequency**: Rewards distributed monthly
- **Payment Method**: Paid in the same token as the staking reward
- **Minimum Threshold**: 10 USD equivalent to claim rewards
- **Auto-Compound Option**: Automatically restake rewards to earn more

## FAQs

### General Questions

**Q: Is there a limit to how many people I can refer?**
A: No, you can refer unlimited users. The more you refer, the higher your tier and commission rate.

**Q: When do I receive my referral rewards?**
A: Rewards are distributed monthly. You can claim them once they reach the minimum threshold of 10 USD equivalent.

**Q: Can I refer myself using a different wallet?**
A: No, self-referrals are prohibited and will result in disqualification from the program.

**Q: What happens if my referee unstakes?**
A: You only earn rewards when your referees are actively earning staking rewards. If they unstake, you won't earn from that particular stake, but they remain your referral for future stakes.

### Tracking & Attribution

**Q: How long does the referral link tracking last?**
A: The referral cookie lasts 30 days. If someone clicks your link and stakes within 30 days, you get credit.

**Q: What if someone clicks multiple referral links?**
A: The first referral link clicked is credited (first-click attribution).

**Q: Can I see who I've referred?**
A: You can see anonymized wallet addresses and their staking activity, but not personal information (privacy-protected).

### Rewards & Payments

**Q: In what tokens do I receive referral rewards?**
A: You receive rewards in the same token that your referee earned staking rewards in (ETH, USDC, USDT, etc.).

**Q: Can I compound my referral rewards?**
A: Yes! You can enable auto-compounding to automatically restake your referral rewards.

**Q: What's the minimum amount I need to claim rewards?**
A: 10 USD equivalent across all tokens. Rewards below this threshold will accumulate until you reach it.

**Q: How are tier upgrades applied?**
A: Tier upgrades are automatic and apply to all future rewards. The bonus commission also applies retroactively to your existing referrals.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Does the referral link work on mobile?**
A: Yes, the referral link works on all devices with compatible Web3 wallets.

**Q: What if the referral link doesn't track properly?**
A: Contact support with the referee's wallet address and transaction hash. We'll manually verify and credit the referral.

**Q: Can I use URL shorteners for my referral link?**
A: Yes, but we recommend using the original link when possible to ensure proper tracking.

### Compliance & Security

**Q: Is the referral program available worldwide?**
A: The program is available in all jurisdictions where VaporFund operates, subject to local regulations.

**Q: How do you prevent referral fraud?**
A: We use advanced analytics to detect suspicious patterns, including self-referrals, wash trading, and bot activity.

**Q: Are my referral earnings secure?**
A: Yes, all rewards are tracked on-chain and secured by smart contracts. You maintain full custody of claimed rewards.
