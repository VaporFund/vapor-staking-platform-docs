## üìä How Earnings Work

### Platform Profit Model

VaporFund earns **~3% annually** on staked funds through DeFi yield strategies.

**Your earnings are calculated as:**

```
Step 1: Platform Profit = Staking Amount √ó 3% √ó Duration (years)
Step 2: Monthly Profit = Annual Profit / 12
Step 3: Month 1 Commission = Monthly Profit √ó 100%
Step 4: Ongoing Commission = Monthly Profit √ó Your Tier Rate (10-40%)
```

### Example Calculation

**User stakes $10,000 for 12 months:**

```
Annual platform net profit: $10,000 √ó 3% = $300
Monthly platform net profit: $300 / 12 = $25/month

Silver Tier (20%):
- Month 1: $25 √ó 100% = $25
- Months 2-12: $25 √ó 20% = $5/month √ó 11 = $55
- Total Year 1: $80
```

## ‚ùì FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is there a cost to join?**
A: No, all programs are completely free. You earn money, never pay.

**Q: When do I get paid?**
A: Monthly on the 1st for Bronze/Silver, weekly for Gold, on-demand for Diamond.

**Q: What's the minimum payout?**
A: $10 for Affiliates, $50 for Partners. Below minimum rolls over to next period.

**Q: Can I be in both programs?**
A: Yes! You can be an affiliate AND a partner simultaneously.

### Commission Questions

**Q: How is the 100% Month 1 bonus calculated?**
A: In the first month after a user stakes, you receive 100% of that month's platform net profit instead of your tier rate.

**Q: Do I earn recurring commissions?**
A: Yes! As long as the user's stake remains active, you earn your tier rate monthly.

**Q: What if a user withdraws early?**
A: You earn commissions only while their stake is active. If they withdraw after 3 months, you earn for 3 months.

**Q: How do I increase my tier?**
A: Reach the referral count or volume threshold for the next tier. Upgrades are automatic.

### Technical Questions

**Q: How is attribution tracked?**
A: Through cookies (30-day window) and wallet address linking on first stake.

**Q: Can I track my earnings in real-time?**
A: Yes, the dashboard shows live earnings, pending payouts, and detailed analytics.

**Q: Do you provide tax documents?**
A: Yes, 1099-MISC for US partners earning $600+/year. International partners receive earning statements.
